about me

hello, my name is Josiah Fox and i am a student at Columbus State Community College. In my free time i love to explore my photography hobby. All the photos on this website are my work! (for a full list of my work, please check out my gallery page)

i have long enjoyed technology and the many practical and artistic applications it plays in our modern lives. Specifically, i enjoy capturing moments in the world as i saw them

one of my favorite places to take photos is at the Columbus Zoo in Ohio, and most of the animal photos were taken there. Most of my shooting is done either with my Canon EOS Rebel T7 DSLR camera or my DJI Mavic Mini (v1). Ive found both to be very performant in the right situation: particularly relative to their excellent affordiblity

because this website is public, much of my personal information is censored and all of the photos are watermarked. It should be noted that none of these photos may be reposted or have their watermark removed under any circumstance apart from my explicit permission
